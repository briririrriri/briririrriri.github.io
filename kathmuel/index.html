About Us

Mission

The mission of “Safety Gaming” is to provide safety tips on online video gaming amidst its harms for safe and fun online video gaming enjoyed by the people.

Vision

Safety Gaming’s vision is to be a channel known for creating a better world for online video games and to make online video games a safe space 

What is "Safety Gaming"?

Our channel which we named “Safety Gaming” helps make change by producing YouTube videos educating people about: How online video games can be harmful to someone’s Mental Health, How can online video gamers prevent addiction to playing online video games, How can they maintain their life and time balanced, and How can they be able to play online video games safely. 

Why "Safety Gaming"?


Our target audience should consider our channel called “Safety Gaming” as we take our mission, vision, and duties seriously, and well. We make sure that all things we share on our channel are reliable. We do not share information that is not valid and is not supported well. We produce products as well with the help of our target audience who are reaching us via our contact details. We are open to everyone. 

The Importance of "Safety Gaming"

Our research topic for English PBA is “The Danger of Online Video Games Among Grade 10 Students of APEC Schools V.Luna’s Mental Health” As we go with our review we discovered the dangers of online video games to one’s mental health from previous studies. Thus, our videos can help by educating online video gamers and their parents on How online video games can be harmful to someone’s Mental Health, How can online video gamers prevent addiction to playing online video games, How can they maintain their life and time balance, and How can they be able to play online video games safely. Grade 10 students of APEC V.Luna can surely benefit as well from this for the reason that they also support e-sports at school.
